About Me

Front-end web developer with a content-writing and poetry background to offer clients full website services. I recently enrolled in and completed a full-stack development “Coding Bootcamp” at The George Washington University, earning a certificate in full-stack development and newly-developed skills in HTML, CSS, Responsive Web Design, JavaScript, jQuery, among others. My goal is to engage users through web and app design for an impactful user experience, as I am a problem-solver, passionate about app and web page building and design. In addition to writing for the We Are Not Numbers organization, I collaborated and worked with its team and developed the organization’s website. I also have independently worked on various projects aimed at developing tools to help users navigate their daily lives, including quizzes, simple games, calculators, and calendars. I look forward to applying the skills I have cultivated to increase web efficiency and enhance the overall user experience.
